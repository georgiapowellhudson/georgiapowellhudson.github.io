My Story

I am a driven sophomore at Syracuse University studying magazine journalism and English, with a minor in sport management. Storytelling has been a pillar of my life since I was a young girl growing up in San Antonio, where I learned the intrinsic value of words through my father, a criminal defense attorney whose prose had the power to alter lives.

I’ve had the opportunity to work at a wide range of publications, from radio at Syracuse’s NPR affiliate station, WAER, to traditional print in the Daily Orange, the university’s independent, student-run paper. Through four semesters at WAER, I’ve gained firsthand experience in the professional world of broadcast news, covering environmental and political stories. At the Daily Orange, I’ve learned how to independently pitch, source and execute stories on a deadline for one of the nation’s premier college sports departments.
